### Hitung Sum CRR Bisnis
$$
\begin{align}
Sum CRR Bisnis &= ScoreOmzet + ScoreHargaBersaing + ScorePersainganPasar + ScoreLokasiUsaha + ScoreProduktivitas + SkorKualitas \\
&= 80 + 70 + 70 + 70 + 70 + 70 \\
&= 420
\end{align}
$$

### Hitung CRR Bisnis
$$
\begin{align}
if \; Rasio Agunan < 30 \% \; then \; Hasil &= 0 \\
else \;  Hasil &= \frac{Sum CRR Bisnis}{6} \\
&= \frac{420}{6} \\
&= 70
\end{align}
$$
